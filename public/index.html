When to Leave

When to Leave is an app that allows you to calculate at what time you need to leave to arrive on time on your destination, given a known travel time.

I developed this app as a high school project. Though I got to admit it's usefulness is limited, it did help me get started on Android development. You can check it out by clicking here.

Gyroblock

Gyroblock is a simple game where you have to use gyroscopic controls to move a block trough an endless loop of obstacles. The more obstacles you avoid the higher your score will be.

Gyroblock was a high school project as well, challenging myself on a new level of application development, since developing a game is quite different from developing a static functional app. You can check it out by clicking here.

Fiets locatie

Fiets locatie, is a simple application (in Dutch), to save the location of your bike. Especially created for saving the location in the train stations.

The motivation for this app was pure laziness, since I think it is quicker to enter the details in my app then to use my notes. Since some other people might share my opinion, I decided to just share the app for anyone who think it's useful. You can check it out by clicking here.
